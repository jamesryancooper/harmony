# Risk Register

Only real repository-specific risks are listed. Each risk states its effect on closure certification.

| Risk ID | Risk | Owner | Trigger | Consequence | Detection method | Mitigation | Effect on certification |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| R-001 | Support universe expansion outruns proof coverage | Governance + Assurance | A host/model/pack is marked admitted before all proof-plane suites are green. | False universal claim and invalid certification. | V-SUP-001, V-ASS-001, V-DISC-002 | No admission without green conformance and proof coverage; keep stage-only until proven. | Certification hard-fails. |
| R-002 | Mission/run migration corrupts historical run semantics | Objective Contract Owners | Bulk migration rewrites run artifacts with lossy or contradictory mission fields. | Broken continuity or false historical provenance. | V-OBJ-001, V-OBJ-002 + diff review | Two-step migration: schema legality dry run, then instance migration with rollback snapshots. | Certification blocked until all run artifacts are legal and traceable. |
| R-003 | Host workflows continue to mint shadow authority | Runtime + CI | A PR or review workflow still creates effective approvals outside canonical artifacts. | Second control plane and unverifiable governance. | V-AUTH-001 plus workflow fixture tests | Move issuance into canonical runtime/governance path; workflows project only. | Certification hard-fails. |
| R-004 | External replay backend is unavailable or unverifiable | Evidence + Runtime | Immutable payload write, hash verification, or restore drill fails. | Replay claim is not honest. | V-EVD-001, V-EVD-002 | Do not certify until backend path, hashes, and restore drills are green; keep claim bounded if unresolved. | Certification blocked. |
| R-005 | Browser/API capability packs widen blast radius unexpectedly | Capability Pack Governance | Pack admission occurs without full allow/stage_only/escalate/deny routing and approvals. | Unsafe action surface and fail-open behavior. | V-CAP-001, V-AUTH-003 | Admit only via governed pack manifests and staged exercises before live release. | Certification hard-fails. |
| R-006 | Legacy persona removal breaks external tool assumptions | Agency + Tooling | External tooling still expects `architect` surfaces. | Broken integrations or confusing onboarding. | V-LEG-001 and mirror tests | Publish adapter notice, keep only non-authoritative archival note if required, and migrate references in same change. | Certification can proceed only if no live runtime dependency remains. |
| R-007 | Hidden human repair remains invisible | Observability + Review | Operators hotfix or rerun outside intervention logging path. | False autonomy/proof claims and unreconstructable history. | V-OBS-001 cross-checks against workflow reruns and merge activity | Instrument all manual touchpoints; reject closure when unexplained touches exist. | Certification hard-fails. |
| R-008 | Behavioral/recovery suites overfit exemplars | Lab + Assurance | New suites pass canned examples but fail held-out scenarios. | Misleading proof-plane coverage. | V-LAB-002, held-out runs, shadow comparisons | Mandatory held-out, shadow, and adversarial packs for each admitted target tuple. | Certification blocked until independent evidence is green. |
| R-009 | Second validation pass introduces drift | Release Engineering | Generated/effective/disclosure artifacts change on the second pass. | Non-idempotent closure and unstable system of record. | V-CERT-001 | Freeze nondeterministic inputs, sort outputs, remove timestamp-only diffs from authoritative artifacts. | Certification hard-fails. |
| R-010 | Mirror adapters drift into shadow policy | Ingress + Tooling | Root or tool-specific adapters gain extra instructions not present in canonical ingress. | Multiple authority surfaces. | V-SOT-002 | Byte-parity or schema-validated projections only, plus drift watch workflow. | Certification blocked. |
| R-011 | Mainline drift invalidates cutover evidence | Release Engineering | Concurrent merges land while cutover branch is being validated. | Evidence no longer matches candidate release. | Cutover branch freshness check in P0/P5 | Freeze merges; rebase emergency fixes into cutover before validation. | Certification delayed until branch is fresh. |
| R-012 | Support-target matrix under-specifies multilingual or extended-governed tiers | Governance | Target universe expansion omits precise admission rules for `extended-governed` or `spanish-secondary`. | Partial portability claim or unsafe broadening. | V-SUP-001 + support-target diff review | State exact admission rules or de-scope explicitly before certification. | Certification blocked if target universe remains ambiguous. |
